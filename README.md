# Trainia

Trainia is a simple web-based **fitness plan generator** that creates personalized workout and nutrition plans based on your goal, available equipment, and weekly training frequency. It's designed to help users reach their fitness goals efficiently and effectively.

---

## Features

- **Personalized Workout Plan:** Generates workouts according to your goal and available equipment. Supports Full Body, Upper/Lower, and Push/Pull/Legs splits depending on your weekly frequency.
- **Nutrition Plan:** Provides meal suggestions tailored to muscle gain, fat loss, or general fitness.
- **Training Tips:** Includes advice like progressive overload, proper form, rest, hydration, and tracking progress.
- **Downloadable Plan:** Option to save your personalized plan as a `.txt` file.

---

## How to Use

1. Open https://trainia.netlify.app/
2. Enter your name, fitness goal, weekly workout frequency, and available equipment.
3. Click **Generate Plan**.
4. Review the workout, nutrition, and training tips.
5. (Optional) Click **Save Plan as TXT** to download your plan.
